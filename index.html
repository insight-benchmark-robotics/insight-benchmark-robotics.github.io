INSIGHT Bench formalizes the task of grounding "in-situ guides" (on-object symbols and text) into manipulation.

Abstract

[cite_start]Humans intuitively rely on text and symbols inscribed on objects (e.g. "PULL", "Squeeze and Turn")[cite: 168]. [cite_start]We introduce INSIGHT Bench, a benchmark that formalizes the task of "in-situ guide grounding"[cite: 171]. [cite_start]It provides a comprehensive taxonomy and a scalable simulation framework for robotic manipulation[cite: 172, 173].

Taxonomy of In-Situ Guides

    • [cite_start]
  • Action Directional Information: Imposes constraints like rotational direction or push/pull[cite: 405].
    • [cite_start]
  • Procedural Guidance: Specifies the sequence for multi-step operations[cite: 408].
